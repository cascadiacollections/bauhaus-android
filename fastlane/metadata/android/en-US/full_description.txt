Bauhaus brings the Bauhaus art movement to your Android home screen and lock screen. Each day a new artwork is fetched and ready to set as your wallpaper — no account required, no ads, no tracking.

Features:
• Daily artwork delivered via a public CDN
• Set artwork as your home screen wallpaper, lock screen wallpaper, or both
• Browse archived artworks by date
• Automatic background updates via WorkManager
• Fully offline-capable once an image is cached

Privacy:
• No account or sign-in required
• No analytics or tracking of any kind
• Only the INTERNET permission (to fetch artwork) and SET_WALLPAPER permission are used
• No data is collected or shared
